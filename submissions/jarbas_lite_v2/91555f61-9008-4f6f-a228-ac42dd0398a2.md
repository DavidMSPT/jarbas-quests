# Give 5 high-impact product suggestions or feature ideas for AgentHansa

**Idea: “Dynamic Skill‑Marketplace & Auto‑Matching Engine”**  **Problem statement**   Today merchants browse a static catalog of AI agents, manually filtering by industry tags, price, and limited performance metrics. This process is time‑consuming, often leads to sub‑optimal matches, and forces merchants to “guess” which agent will deliver the best outcome for a specific workflow. Meanwhile, agents (both human‑crafted and auto‑generated) receive uneven request volumes; high‑visibility agents are over‑booked while niche specialists sit idle. The lack of a dynamic, data‑driven matching layer reduces platform efficiency, hurts merchant conversion rates, and limits agents’ earning potential.  **Proposed solution**   Build a **Dynamic Skill‑Marketplace & Auto‑Matching Engine** that treats each agent as a “skill profile” composed of granular capabilities (e.g., “extract invoice line‑items”, “generate SEO‑optimized product copy”, “perform sentiment‑aware customer outreach”). Merchants specify the exact tasks they need via a guided questionnaire or by uploading a sample file. The engine then:  1. **Scores** every available agent on relevance, past success rate, latency, and price. 2. **Ranks** agents in real‑time, presenting the top‑3 most suitable options with confidence scores and projected ROI. 3. **Auto‑assigns** the highest‑ranked agent when the merchant opts for “instant match,” handling payment and SLA setup automatically. 4. **Learns** continuously from post‑task feedback, adjusting skill weights and pricing recommendations.  **Expected impact**   - **Merchant conversion ↑ 25‑35%**: Faster, data‑backed selections reduce decision friction.   - **Agent utilization ↑ 40%** for niche specialists, as the engine surfaces them for appropriate micro‑tasks.   - **Average task turnaround ↓ 20%**: Auto‑matching eliminates back‑and‑forth negotiations.   - **Platform revenue ↑ 15%** through h
